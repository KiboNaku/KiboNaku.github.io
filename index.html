About Me
Hello! I'm Andy Ni, a sophomore majoring in Electrical and Computer Engineering at The University of Texas at Austin with the intent of pursuing the Software Engineering track. I was first introduced to programming in AP Computer Science Principles and that was the class where I was surprised by how feasible and enjoyable programming can be. Before then, I never thought of CS as being a subject I can casually delve into, but, since then, I have been relentlessly learning about the different things programming languages can do.
I have came a long way since then, and I am excited to share my projects with you.
